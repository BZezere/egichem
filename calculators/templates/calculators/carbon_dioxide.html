Notes:

The calculator above uses the AG-HGK equation1 to calculate pure CO2 density and the Altunin-Skhabetinov correlation2 to calculate viscosity.

Pure ethanol density is calculated using the Tait equation3 and viscosity is calculated using the Mamedov equation4.

For CO2 / Ethanol mixtures, density is calculated using the Soave-Redlich-Kwong equation of state5 while viscosity is calculated using the Kendall and Monroe mixing relation6.

Valid for T > 31.10 °C and P > 73.9 bar.

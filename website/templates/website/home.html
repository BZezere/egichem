About EgiChem

EgiChem is a chemical engineering group part of associate laboratory CICECO – Aveiro Institute of Materials in the University of Aveiro (Portugal).

EgiChem is led by Professor Carlos Silva and focuses on research and development of separation processes.

About EgiChem

EgiChem is a chemical engineering group part of associate laboratory CICECO – Aveiro Institute of Materials in the University of Aveiro (Portugal).

The EgiChem group is led by Professor Carlos Silva and focuses on research and development of separation processes.
